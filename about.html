About Us

Business Terms of Crown Nova Technologies LTD

Crown Nova Technologies is a trusted IT technical service provider, specializing in on-demand and well-managed IT support services. Our engineers operate with the precision and dedication of soldiers—ready to be deployed to client sites and swiftly resolve technical issues. We maintain a dedicated team of expert Field Engineers, available around the clock for on-site support. Our in-house technical team carefully assesses each issue and assigns the most suitable engineer with the required skill set, ensuring efficient and effective resolution. At Crown Nova, we are committed to delivering top-tier Field Engineer (FE) management services and assembling the best IT field teams to meet client demands with excellence and reliability.

What We Do

We Provide Flexible IT Support Services

Our highly skilled technical team is available 24/7 to provide on-site support and resolve issues swiftly and efficiently. We offer unlimited on-site user support through a network of dedicated and experienced engineers who deliver both professional services and expert guidance. Our certified professionals adhere to industry best practices, ensuring that every solution aligns with business needs and operational goals. Whether it's routine maintenance or urgent troubleshooting, we are committed to providing reliable, top-quality IT support around the clock.

We are Offering

Our Services to World's Best Giant IT Companies

We are a global IT support provider, offering expert services across a wide range of leading technologies, including HP, IBM, Dell, Cisco, Fujitsu, NetApp, EMC, Oracle/Sun, Hitachi, and more. Our comprehensive service offerings include hardware maintenance support, IMAC (Install, Move, Add, Change) services, smart hands support for desktops, servers, storage systems, tape libraries, and detailed site surveys. Our Head Office is based in London, UK, with a global presence supported by a network of experienced engineers and strategic business partners.
